Our Story

Hilltoppers Adventures was founded in 2011 by a group of outdoor educators who believed that every young person deserves the opportunity to experience the transformative power of nature.

What started as weekend hiking trips for a handful of local youth has grown into a comprehensive outdoor education program serving hundreds of young people each year across multiple activities and seasons.

Today, we continue our commitment to making outdoor adventure accessible to all youth, regardless of background or financial circumstances, while building the skills and confidence they need to become lifelong outdoor enthusiasts.

Our Impact

Over the past 15 years, Hilltoppers Adventures has served more than 5,000 young people through our programs. Our alumni report increased confidence, better academic performance, and a lasting connection to nature.

We're proud that 100% of participants who need financial assistance receive it, ensuring that cost is never a barrier to outdoor adventure.

Many of our alumni have gone on to pursue careers in outdoor education, environmental science, and conservation, carrying forward the values they learned with us.
